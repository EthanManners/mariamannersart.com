About

My name is Maria Manners, and I am a versatile artist and illustrator with a background in Architecture. I hold Master's degrees in both Visual Development and Illustration from the Academy of Art University in San Francisco.

Based in my studio in Austin, Texas, I specialize in creating characters, environments, and props. I also illustrate stories and surface collections using both traditional and digital mediums.

Please feel free to contact me to discuss your potential project.
